In this section you will see my portfolio, it will mainly consist of my clothing, you'll be able to see some of the pieces I've made. Each piece I make is a 1 of 1, each person gets a slightly different piece, and they have the ability to ask for it to be customized. When creating my clothing I like to go for a darker more grunge look, and that's what you can see here!

I've been making these clothes since 2017, and I've continued to make them ever since,I do it less now due to college and work, but it's a great creative outlet on the side! When I was really pursuing clothing we hit 2,000 followers on instagram and we were doing photo shoots and things of that nature, covid-19 really through everything off though, blank clothes were harder to get, and meeting up for photoshoots was a no go during the early 2020 lockdown, so i shifted my focus to school and the IT field!
